About the Calcs

These calculators were made by others and the original code on some has been modified to fix errors, work on one page, change asthetics, and minor tweaks for additional info.

Adria(staves without spells) cannot sell items with affixes that are higher than half of the lowest qlvl. Premium Item Checker does not account for that. It also does not account for Bountiful and Plentiful only existing in tandem with spells. Familiarize yourself with the formulas in Jarulf's Guide to be sure of the results from these calculators.

- Ghast
