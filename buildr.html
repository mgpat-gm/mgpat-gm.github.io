Rogue Builds by FireIceTalon

FireIceTalon has been playing since the beginning and is likely the most experienced PvP and PvM player of Diablo. This is a graphical representation with his original descriptions of the builds he has put together. Originally listed on The Horadrim discord server. Of note: You can substitute Obsidian for Emerald in cases where you can reach MAX resists with Obsidian.

Helm: Thinking Cap

Weapon: King's Sword of Haste (Alt: Strange Sword of Haste)
Shield: Topaz Shield of Blocking (note: must be 15%)

This is a tough duel for the melee Rogue, but this build combines a higher splvl of 17 combined with effective stunlock to make this setup dangerous all around. Harmony and fast block are a must, as is max resists. King's Sword of Haste should provide more than enough to hit vs most Bow Rogues, but Strange Sword of Haste will get the job done if the Bow Rogue opts for high ac. This build can also be effective vs Warriors.

It is recommended you have fast block and max resists in this duel, whatever you decide to use.
